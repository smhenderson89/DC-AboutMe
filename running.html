He's A Little Runaway

I've been running on and off for the past 4 years. I first started running while at Orange Theory (Orange theory is like a spin class on steorids).

After feeling more comfortable getting in the rythym of running, I started running outdoors. I never knew how good it feels to run shirtless with the wind blowing past you. It's one of the best feelings in the world.

My first entries into races lead me to run in the Moonlight Run, a annual race that is held at night with only the light of the moon to guide you through a 5k and 10k. Running at night with only a headlamp to light your way was magical, and I was hooked to try more challenging races.

This culminated with my 2020 new year's resolution to run a half marathon. While the actual race was canceled due to Covid-19, I was able to run the race virtually by myself and finish in a time of 2 hours, 47 minutes, and 6 seconds. I have gained a some weight recently but I am hoping to get back into shape for running.
